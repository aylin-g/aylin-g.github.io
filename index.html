My Projects

Scientific Graphics: Inflammatory Processes in Gluten-Containing Grains

Gluten-containing grains, such as wheat, barley, and rye, can trigger inflammatory processes in susceptible individuals. This is particularly significant for those with celiac disease and non-celiac gluten sensitivity. In my work, I utilize scientific graphics to illustrate these complex mechanisms, effectively visualizing how gluten interacts with the immune system.
